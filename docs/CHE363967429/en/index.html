Company Profile

Real estate with a clear view of ownership, development and execution.

Ops SPV AG is a Swiss stock corporation focused on real estate of all kinds. Its profile centers on acquiring, holding, managing and selectively selling property assets.

The company's purpose also includes the planning, development and execution of new buildings and conversions, linking portfolio work with practical development capability.

A lean structure for the full real estate lifecycle.
